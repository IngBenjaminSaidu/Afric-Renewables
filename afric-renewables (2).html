Who We Are
Rooted in Africa.
Built for Africa.

We believe every African community deserves affordable, clean, and reliable energy. AFRIC RENEWABLES was founded to close the energy gap — not with charity, but with world-class engineering, local expertise, and a genuine long-term commitment to the continent.

We hire and train African engineers and technicians at every level. We design systems for Africa's unique climate, geography, and economic realities. And we stay — operating and maintaining for decades, not just building and leaving.

How We Work
Our 4-Step Delivery Model

From first site visit to decades of operation — here is how AFRIC RENEWABLES brings a project to life.

01

Assess & Design

Site surveys, resource mapping, load analysis, and system design tailored to the community's exact needs and budget.

02

Finance & Permit

Project finance structuring, regulatory approvals, grid connection agreements, and deep community engagement.

03

Build & Commission

Local procurement where possible, construction management, equipment testing, and full grid commissioning.

04

Operate & Optimise

Remote monitoring 24/7, preventive maintenance schedules, performance analytics, and continuous improvement.
